The Insect Hashira

Age: 18
Height: 4'11" (151 cm)

Appearance:
Lavender-tinted black hair tied with butterfly clips
Violet eyes
Butterfly-themed haori with gradient purple and blue edges

Abilities & Powers:
Enhanced Speed: Among the fastest of the Hashira with precise movements
Poison Expertise: Wields wisteria-based poison to kill demons
Modified Nichirin Blade: A needle-like sword designed for stabbing and injecting poison
Insect Breathing: Agile and fast combat style
Sharp Intelligence: Excels in strategy and demon anatomy

Personality:
Calm, cheerful, and gentle on the surface, but harbors a deep hatred for demons due to her sister’s death.
